ABOUT THE EVENT

hackCBS is India's Biggest Student-run Hackathon, with a vision to provide a recognized platform to the tech enthusiasts. hackCBS seeks to cumulate actioN oriented learning and a competitive environment for all.

It has experienced an exponential growth in terms of reach and participation along the way through each edition. hackCBS was the first ever event of its kind which marked the outset of hackathons. hackCBS 2.0 was Asia's first ever MLH member hackathon, succeeded by two digitally organized editions as hackCBS 3.0 and hackCBS 4.0 with an overwhelming response from all over the world and over 2500 registrations.

Despite the pandemic, the team pulled off the last couple of editions with great vigor and zeal and is motivated to set new standards this year with hackCBS 5.0.
